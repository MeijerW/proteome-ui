ProteomeUI

Interactive exploration of spatial and spatiotemporal gene expression during somitogenesis

ProteomeUI allows researchers to explore RNA and protein expression patterns across embryonic spatial domains and developmental timepoints.

  • Spatial RNA / Protein expression
  • Spatiotemporal gene dynamics
  • Multi-gene clustering heatmaps
  • Download publication-quality figures
Launch Explorer

About the Dataset

This tool integrates transcriptomic and proteomic measurements from somitogenesis experiments. Expression values are visualized as Z-scores across spatial and temporal developmental contexts.

Developed by Wilke Meijer, PhD, Sonnen lab (2026) Part of "Spatiotemporal proteomics reveals dynamic antagonistic gradients shaping signalling waves, Meijer et al, (2026), 10.1101/2025.09.05.674076"
