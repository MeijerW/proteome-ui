ProteomeUI

Interactive exploration of RNA and protein expression during somitogenesis

Launch Explorer

About

ProteomeUI enables interactive exploration of spatial and spatiotemporal gene expression patterns. The platform integrates RNA sequencing and quantitative proteomics datasets across embryonic developmental stages.

Features

  • Spatial RNA vs Protein expression
  • Spatiotemporal gene dynamics
  • Multi-gene clustering heatmaps
  • Publication-ready figure export
