ProteomeUI

Interactive exploration of RNA and protein expression during mouse somitogenesis

Launch Explorer

About

ProteomeUI enables interactive exploration of spatial and spatiotemporal gene expression patterns in somitogenesis, without any need for coding experience. The platform integrates our published RNA sequencing and proteomics datasets, allowing users to visualize and analyze gene expression dynamics during somitogenesis. Spatiotemporal proteomics reveals dynamic antagonistic gradients shaping signalling waves Wilke H. M. Meijer, Virginia Andrade, Suzan Stelloo, Wouter M. Thomas, Marek J. van Oostrom, Eveline F. Ilcken, Kim T. J. Peters, Michiel Vermeulen, Katharina F. Sonnen bioRxiv 2025.09.05.674076; doi: https://doi.org/10.1101/2025.09.05.674076 This website was built by Wilke Meijer, PhD in the Sonnen lab at the Hubrecht Institute. It is open-source and available on GitHub.

Features

  • Spatial RNA vs Protein expression
  • Spatiotemporal RNA and Protein dynamics
  • Multi-gene clustering heatmaps
  • Publication-ready figure export
